This letter is in response to your request for a Letter of Determination regardingtheproperty at 1167-1169 Mission
Street ("subject property"). The request letter  states that a  Licensed  Clinical  Social  Worker Professional
Corporation, Inc. (d.b.a. "HOPE Program") iscurrently a service provider for the State of California, Department of
Corrections and Rehabilitation (CDCR), with an office located at 1167 Mission Street, Suite 200 (subject property).
The request letter seeks confirmation that counseling and psychotherapy services, including sex offender
treatment, to probationers, parolees, and private clients are permitted activities at the subject property. Further,
the request letter seeks confirmation that the Planning Department does not have an active record of any
violations of sanitation, building, health, and safety codes at subject property.

The subject property is located within the Mixed Use-Office (MUO) Zoning District, subject to the use controls of
Planning Code Section 842. Counseling services fall within the definition of Assembly and Social Service (an
Institutional Use). Pursuant to Planning Code Section 890.50(a), Assembly and Social Service Use is defined as a
use which provides social, fraternal, counseling, or recreational gathering services to the community. It includes a
private noncommercial clubhouse, lodge, meeting hall, family or district association, recreation building, or
community facility not publicly owned. It also includes an unenclosed recreation area.

Determination.
Pursuant to PlanningCode Sections 842.34 and 890.50(a), Assembly and Social Service (an Institutional Use) uses
are permitted at the subject property. Further, there are no active Planning Department complaints or Department
of Building Inspection complaints on the subject property.
